Let's know more about us

Why Us

Continuing the legacy of the progressive education movement, Srirampura Government Primary School prepares students to be informed, courageous, and compassionate people.

About Us

Srirampura Government School recognizes that every child is unique and is committed to helping each student understand their own power to make positive change in their world.

Activities

Our Belonging curriculum is specifically designed for grades 6-9 and teaches skills and concepts that make life more meaningful (in class and beyond!). Middle school students learn to explore who they are.
